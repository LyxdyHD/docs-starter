## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights. VexRender complies with standard GDPR and CCPA practices.

## Data We Collect

- Account data: email address (for login and support).
- Usage data: prompts, parameters, feature usage, and diagnostics to improve the product.

## Image and Video Retention

- Generated assets are stored on temporary cloud storage (Cloudflare R2).
- Assets are automatically and permanently deleted after 24 hours unless you explicitly publish them to the Explore page.

## Payments

- Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.

## Third Parties

- We use Google Cloud Vertex AI to generate content. Prompt and related data may be sent to Vertex AI solely for processing your requests.
- We use Firebase Auth for authentication and session management.

## Cookies and Tracking

- We use cookies for authentication (Firebase Auth) and to maintain sessions.

## Legal Bases (GDPR)

- Consent: where you opt in (e.g., publishing to Explore).
- Contract: to provide and improve the service you request.
- Legitimate interests: product security, fraud prevention, and analytics.

## Your Rights

- Access, correction, deletion, and portability of your data, subject to applicable law.
- Request account deletion by emailing: `founder@vexrender.com`.

## Data Security

- We use industry‑standard measures to protect data in transit and at rest, but no system is completely secure.

## International Transfers

- Data may be processed in regions where our providers operate. We use contractual safeguards where required.

## Data Retention Summary

- Account data: retained while your account is active.
- Usage data: retained as needed to operate and improve the service.
- Generated assets: auto‑deleted after 24 hours unless published.

## Children

- VexRender is for users 18+ and is not directed to children.

## Changes

- We may update this policy to reflect changes in law or product features. We will post updated versions in the documentation.

## Contact

- Privacy questions: `founder@vexrender.com`.
