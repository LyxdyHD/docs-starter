## Overview

Improve clarity and sharpness while preserving textures and edges. Ideal for social exports or print-ready assets.

## Controls

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Scale" icon="magnifying-glass-plus">
    2× or 4× upscaling.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Detail" icon="eye">
    Balance between smoothness and micro-detail.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Face restore" icon="user">
    Optionally enhance facial features.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Artifact reduction" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Minimize compression noise and halos.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Workflow

- Upscale final selections after generation
- Compare detail settings side-by-side before exporting
- Use lower artifact reduction for clean sources; higher for compressed images
