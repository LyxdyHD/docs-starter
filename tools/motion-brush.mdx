## Overview

Paint motion onto areas of a video to add dynamic effects without affecting the entire frame.

## Controls

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Brush" icon="paintbrush">
    Size and softness for precise selection.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Motion" icon="arrows-spin">
    Direction, speed, and intensity.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mask" icon="layer-group">
    Add, subtract, and refine regions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Preview" icon="play">
    Quick preview before committing changes.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Workflow

- Identify the region and paint the selection with a soft brush
- Choose direction and speed to match scene dynamics
- Preview and iterate until movement feels natural
- Export or continue editing with style or upscaling tools
