## Overview

Create AI-generated videos from text prompts or image sequences, with control over motion, duration, and style.

## Key controls

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Prompt" icon="film">
    Describe motion, scene progression, and visual style.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Duration" icon="clock">
    Set clip length and frame rate.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Source" icon="images">
    Start from a still image or multi-image storyboard.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stabilization" icon="anchor">
    Reduce jitter and maintain subject continuity.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Workflow

- Draft a short storyboard or outline the motion beats
- Choose duration and frame rate suited for your platform
- Generate multiple takes and compare continuity, focus, and pacing
- Use Motion Brush to refine specific regions post-generation

## Export

- Download as MP4 or WebM
- Choose aspect ratios for social, web, or landscape formats
