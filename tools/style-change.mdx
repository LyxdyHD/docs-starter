## Overview

Apply style transfers to images or videos to match branding or creative direction without altering core composition.

## Controls

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Style preset" icon="palette">
    Choose from curated styles or your saved presets.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Intensity" icon="sliders">
    Blend between original and target style.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Structure fidelity" icon="border-all">
    Keep subjects and layout intact.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Batch" icon="layer-group">
    Apply across multiple assets for consistency.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Workflow

- Select a base asset and choose a style preset or reference
- Adjust intensity to avoid oversaturation or loss of detail
- Use structure fidelity to protect key regions and faces
- Save the preset for future campaigns
